<p>OLN 360 is a programme unique to CHIJ OLN where our P3 to P6 students explore and experience success in a range of authentic activities, apply learning beyond the classroom, develop their Learning Dispositions and grow in our school’s GRACIA values. This 4-year programme is delivered via 4 modules each year.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/SpecialProg/360Infographic_w.jpeg"></div><h4>Have a Heart!
