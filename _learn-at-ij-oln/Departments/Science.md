<h2><strong>Science Department</strong></h2><h3>Key Programmes</h3><h4>Science Projects</h4><p>Science projects provide a platform for students to enjoy learning Science and encourages curiosity. Through science projects, Primary 3 to Primary 5 students are given opportunities to pursue a topic that interests them and to demonstrate the ability to communicate scientific knowledge. </p><p>Students work in groups to choose suitable materials, design and present their experiment as well as the process they used to come up with a final product.
